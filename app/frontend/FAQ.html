Frequently Asked Questions

Aren't there already other (VR-)table top simulators out there?

  DFD aims to be the MOST immersive tabletop EVER. This means we will give players features they never had before while playing tabletop, while at the same time making it easier than ever to play tabletop:
 Become one with your hero character by making your own 3D-Character that you can control
Not only talk about your worlds but actually dive inside them, walk around, explore and interact with your friends inside them
Take your combat to the next level by possessing your miniature: no more spectating, dive into the battle!
Choose from a WIDE variety of 3D battlemaps, modify them or make your own!
Actually speak to all the magical creatures of your adventures. Your gamemaster doesn't have to pretend anymore. He can transform into any NPC he wants you to meet. 
and much more....  

Why should I support the project right now instead of waiting for the release?

  We created a Kickstarter project for DFD because we want the community to be part of the development. If you support us now, not only will you get Kickstarter EXCLUSIVE rewards (and heffty rebates!). But you will also become part of the development process of DFD. YOUR input will help us make the best version of DFD: the version YOU want to play. All Kickstarter supporters can exchange with us on our Discord and give input on features, assets or content, that we will add to DFD.  

Can I also play this game whitout a VR Headset?

Yes, DFD supports traditional mouse and keyboard input AND VR-devices!  This means, that you will be able to play with all of your friends with or without VR-headset!    

What rulesets do you support? Can I also play other TableTop RPGs with DFD?

You can play any tabletop RPG that you want! You can easily import any character sheets or notes into DFD and we make sure, that you can modify all the elements in DFD to fit your liking. Furthermore, DFD will include game mechanics and stat integration for DnD 5e on launch. That means that things like initiative tracking can be done with just a click of a button. 

Will You also offer prebuilt campaigns in DFD? 

Yes! If you don't have time to write your own campaigns, you can pick one of our prebuilt campaigns. With our carefully written stories, NPCs and even encounters your Gamemaster can host a thrilling gamenight even without any preparation. You can also modify our campaigns to make your own, unique adventures! Or write your own campaigns of course!

Will you support other genres like steampunk, sci-fi or cyberpunk?

We want to give players ALL the tools they need for their tabletop adventures. So that you can experience your own, unique adventures. If that means the community wants to have steampunk or sci-fi assets, we will 100% give that to them. DFD will never stop evolving and continuously add more free content for the players even after release. So while we currently only plan to support the fantasy settings at launch, additional settings are definitely on our radars for the after launch roadmap!    

What does "lifelong license" mean? Will DFD become a subscription later?

No. We wanted to emphasize that we are not using a subscription model, but we might have formulated it a bit confusingly. If you buy DFD now, you'll be able to play it forever. If you buy it later, you will be able to play it forever.

Will you support the Oculus Quest/ Quest 2?

DFD can be played with any VR Headset! For now, if you want to use the Quest you can simply connect it via link to your laptop or PC. And even better: After the full release, native Quest support is one of the first things on our planned feature agenda, that we will look into!

Will the game support other races such as dwarves, elves, orcs, etc ?

For the launch of the game, we will start with a wide variety of base characters from all kind of races to choose from. Here all the humans will be customizable. For "fancy" races, we will add customization later down the road (depending on the amount of resources/backers we are able to get).

What's the character sheet support like? Is there built-in support for game systems ?

Yes! If you play 5e stats are integrated and you can use the ingame UI to create and track your character stats! But of course, we support all the rulesets. So if you have a character sheet lying around you can just import the picture file. It's that easy :)
