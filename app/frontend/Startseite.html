Dungeon Full Dive: The new way to play tabletop

What is DFD?

DFD is a Pc game (with crossplay between Non-VR and VR-Players!) that lets you actually be INSIDE your tabletop world! May it be a forest deep inside the kingdom of elves, a dark, mysterious dungeon, or just a small tavern for the adventurers to rest. Create your own adventures in DFD!  

EXPLORE

Your Game Master can choose from a wide variety of worlds for the party: from mystical forests to cozy taverns, snowy mountains lost dungeons, and many more. But that's not all: we will continuously create more free worlds after release for the players to explore!

ROLEPLAY

 Have you ever wanted to actually BE the hero in your adventures? No more describing, pick your hero and dive into your adventure. Your friends don't have to imagine that you are a half-orc if you...well... actually are a half-orc. 

COMBAT

In DFD you can place a complete building on your table as a battlemap: no problem. And the best part? Players can actually switch into the eyes of their miniatures with the press of a button. Just don't get scared when you face your first dragon in DFD...
